Hands-Free Driving: A Game-Changer

During the demo day, I tested Ford’s BlueCruise on a Mustang Mach-E, and it felt like magic. The car handled lane changes and curves on its own, only asking me to keep my eyes on the road. Here’s what I’ve seen in advanced driver assistance:

  • Ford BlueCruise: Hands-free on mapped highways, with automatic lane changes.
  • Tesla Autopilot: Neural network-driven lane keeping and exit navigation.
  • GM Super Cruise: Hands-free with driver monitoring, perfect for long drives.

These systems don’t replace drivers but make long trips less tiring. They’re a glimpse into the future of autonomy.
